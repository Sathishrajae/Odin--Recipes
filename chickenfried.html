1.Chicken Rice

Chicken Fried Rice, most popular and loved rice dish in and around the world. Every parts of the world has their own way of making chicken fried rice. This is a quick version of chicken fried rice made with chicken and vegetables very similar to take out fried rice. It is best to use day old rice for making fried rice just like my egg fried rice and prawn fried rice. Learn how to make chicken fried rice with step by step pictures and video.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups cooked white rice, cold
  • 1 cup cooked, chopped chicken meat
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Making Method

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Beat egg and water together in small bowl.
  3. Add egg and cook without stirring, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Remove egg from skillet and cut into shreds;set aside.
  5. Heat oil in the same skillet.
  6. Add onion,cook and stir until soft, about 3 minutes.
  7. Stir in rice, chicken, soy sauce, and pepper.
  8. Cook and stir for about 5 minutes; stir in shredded egg.
  9. Serve hot.
