2.Chicken Fry

Easy chicken fry recipe: a simple and basic recipe for making chicken fry using cubed boneless chicken pieces and spices. There are probably thousands of different variations to an easy chicken fry recipe including what you can add to the marinade, the quantity of spices, whether you use pepper powder or not, etc.

Ingredients

  • 250 grams cubed boneless chicken
  • 1.5 tsp fresh ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tsp lime juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Making Method

  1. Clean the chicken pieces and place in a bowl
  2. Add all the spices, ginger garlic paste, salt, and lime juice to this
  3. Mix well until the chicken pieces are well coated. You can also mix the marinade ingredients first in the bowl and then add the chicken pieces and mix together to coat
  4. Cover and refrigerate for a few hours or overnight. Leave for a minimum of 30 minutes if you are short on time
  5. Heat oil for deep frying and gently add the chicken pieces to it without overcrowding them
  6. Fry on medium-heat heat stirring occasionally until the chicken pieces are golden brown
  7. Take out one piece and test to see if the pieces are fully cooked
  8. When done, drain and place on kitchen towels for the excess oil to get absorbed
  9. Proceed with the remaining pieces until all done
